Welcome!

Vernonia Golf Club is a public golf course nestled in the foothills of the Oregon coast range along the upper Nehalem River.Opened in 1928, our tree lined fairways and well-manicured greens make this course an enjoyable and memorable experience for golfers of all levels.

Our Mission
Under new ownership as of May 2012, we will strive to accommodate all your golf needs. Our new staff, inviting atmosphere, competitive rates and personalized menu options are guaranteed to exceed group needs and requests. We will provide a memorable experience for your golf, tournament, banquet or event. And our service is guaranteed with a smile.
