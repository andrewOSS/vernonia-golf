More than just Golf

Vernonia Golf Club strives to be more than just your average golf course. Along with golf, we offer a wide variety of services that are sure to fit your needs and an individual or as a group such as golf lessons for people of all experience levels.
Make sure you check out our Facebook page for upcoming special events we have planned for you.

Weddings

A wedding is that special time in everyone's life where friends, loved ones, and families are brought together to share in the love of a happy couple ready to start their new lives together. For an event this special, Vernonia Golf Club is the perfect place to have fun, enjoy beautiful scenery, and make the special day even better.

Family Functions

Everyone has things in their lives that they love to celebrate. Birthdays, graduations, and family reunions are all events that need plenty of space to invite everyone and celebrate. The Vernonia Golf Club is happy to provide all the room you need for your special occasions.

And More!

Business meetings, night golfing, and tournaments are all things Vernonia Golf Club can offer. The list of services we can provide goes beyond can be typed on a website, so feel free to contact us if you have any plans or events you'd like a location for, or if you have any questions.
